The Video Game Olympics

How it Works

  • Round 1
    Stations
  • Round 2
    Bracket
  • Round 3
    The Finals

Stations

  • 1. You will be assigned to either A group or B group upon ticket purchase.
  • 2. Later, you will be assigned to 1 of 10 stations.
  • 3. Each station will have 2 lines, marked A or B. Stand in the line that corresponds to your assigned group.
  • 4. Wait until it is your turn to play the game at your station, then play the game.
  • 5. If you win, you will receive a punch in your game slip.
  • 6. Rotate to the closest game station.
    • Group A rotates left.
    • Group B rotates right.
  • 7. Continue rotating and playing games until you have played 5 games.

Bracket

  • 1. Look at your Game Slip to determine your status in the upcoming Bracket Round.
    • If you have less than 3 wins from the Stations Round, you are out of the Video Game Olympics.
    • If you have 3 or more wins from the Stations Round, you may be given a "by" with participants with the most wins getting the priority.
    • Remember, the more wins you have from the Bracket Round, the higher the chance you get to skip a round to get closer to the Finals.
  • 2. Play in the bracket until you lose. From here on out, if you lose a game you are out of the VGO's.

The Finals

  • 1. Losers of the Semi-Final Round play for 3rd.
  • 2. Winners of the Semi-Final Round play for 1st and 2nd place.
